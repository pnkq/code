About

Welcome to my home page. I'm the head of the Data Science Laboratory at the Faculty of Mathematics, Mechanics and Informatics, College of Science, Vietnam National University in Hanoi. I am working as a senior research scientist in some research projects of FPT Corp., a counselor for al+, a Japanese startup company in artificial intelligence. I am also an associate member of the Data Science Laboratory of the VIASM (Vietnam Institute for Advanced Study in Mathematics).

My research centers on natural language processing (NLP) and its applications in computer science, mostly: parsing algorithms, corpus-based NLP, grammar development, tree adjoining grammars, statistical methods for NLP and their applications. I am particularly interested in the processing of Vietnamese.

My education in brief

  • 2010, PhD of computer science, LORIA - France (Laboratoire Lorrain de Recherche en Informatique et ses Applications)
  • 2005, MS of information technology, IFI - Hanoi, Vietnam (Institut de la Francophonie pour l'Informatique)
  • 2002, BAC of applied mathematics, College of Science, Vietnam National University, Hanoi
